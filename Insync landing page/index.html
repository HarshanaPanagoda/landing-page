    Get To Know More

    InSync is an AI-powered voice-based assistant used to communicate with customers instead of relying on live operators for customer service. The AI will quickly translate the customer’s spoken words into text, analyses the content, generate a text response, and convert it into a vocal response for the customer. If the AI is unable to address the customer’s query, the call will automatically transfer to a live operator.

    This AI-driven voice application allows customers to effortlessly report issues via voice, ensuring seamless 24/7 service and tailored solutions that account for the customer's emotional state. This approach acknowledges the significance of human conversation and voice in customer interactions, aiming to enhance customer satisfaction and revolutionize customer support in the banking industry.


    Features

    InSync boasts a sophisticated voice assistant feature that enables customers to interact with the application using natural language. Customers can speak their queries, and the AI-powered system will swiftly translate their words into text, analyze the content, and generate a relevant response. This feature eliminates the need for customers to navigate complex menus or type out their concerns, enhancing convenience and accessibility. .

    Live Operator Transfer

    If the AI-powered system is unable to address a customer's query satisfactorily, InSync seamlessly transfers the call to a live operator. This ensures that customers always receive the support they need, even for complex or nuanced issues. By combining AI-driven automation with human expertise, InSync offers a comprehensive support solution that caters to diverse customer needs

    Loan Calculations

    Another key feature of InSync is its ability to perform loan calculations quickly and accurately. Customers can input relevant data, such as loan amount, interest rate, and repayment term, and InSync will generate detailed loan repayment schedules. This empowers customers to make informed financial decisions and better understand the implications of various loan options

    Query Reporting

    InSync facilitates seamless reporting of customer queries directly to the bank as a third party. By consolidating and categorizing customer feedback, InSync provides valuable insights that banks can use to improve their products and services continuously. This feature fosters a proactive approach to customer relationship management, allowing banks to address issues promptly and enhance overall service quality

    Map Integration

    InSync integrates mapping functionality to assist customers in locating nearby bank branches or ATMs. Whether customers need to make a deposit, withdraw cash, or access other banking services, they can rely on InSync to provide accurate and up-to-date location information. This feature enhances convenience for customers on the go, ensuring they can access banking services with ease
